\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implementation of the elastic free energy function $\rho_0 \Psi^\mathrm{mech} = \dfrac{\mu^\mathrm{e}}{2} [F_{ij} F_{ij} - 3 - 2 \ln(J) ] + \dfrac{\mu^\mathrm{i}}{2} {U^\mathrm{i}}^{-1}_{ik} F_{lk} F_{lm} {U^\mathrm{i}}^{-1}_{mi} $, where ${U^\mathrm{i}}^{-1}_{kl}$ is symmetric, while $F_{kl}$ is unsymmetric, $J$ is the determinant of $F_{kl}$, and $\mu^\mathrm{e}>0$ and $\mu^\mathrm{i}>0$ are material parameters.
